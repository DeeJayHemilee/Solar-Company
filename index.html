Who We Are?

Empowering Africa with Clean, Reliable Energy

CEERIDA is an African-owned renewable energy company committed to unlocking the continent’s vast clean energy potential. With a focus on solar, storage, and sustainable infrastructure, we work with governments, NGOs, and private sector partners to create energy access where it matters most.


We bridge the energy gap with scalable, impactful solutions designed specifically for Africa’s diverse environments—from rural villages to growing cities.
